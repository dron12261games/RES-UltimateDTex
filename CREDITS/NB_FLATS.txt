Title                   : Useful flats

Filename                : nb_flats.zip (nb_flats.txt and nb_flats.wad)

Author                  : Nick Baker
		  
Email Address           : nick@frad.org

Description             : A collection of around 50 flats which almost

			  rotated versions of existing flats, etc.
